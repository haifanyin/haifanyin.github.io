Short Bio:

Haifan Yin received his Ph.D. degree from Télécom ParisTech (also known as École Nationale Supérieure des Télécommunications, or ENST), France, in Dec. 2015, under the supervision of Prof. David Gesbert. He received his B.S. and M.S. degrees from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2009 and 2012 respectively. From 2016 to 2017, he has been a DSP engineer in Sequans Communications - an IoT chipmaker based in Paris, France. From 2017 to 2019, he has been a senior research engineer in Shanghai Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd., where he contributed a lot to physical layer research and 5G standardizaiton. Since May 2019, he has joined the School of Electronic Information and Communications at Huazhong University of Science and Technology as a full professor. Dr. Yin has published 15 journal and conference papers. One of his technical papers reaches over 800 citations. He is also a holder of 15 patents. His research interests include wireless communications, signal processing, machine learning, etc.
